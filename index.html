Textarea2 Demo

A better plaintext input, implemented as a web component. Learn more on GitHub.

Above is the textarea with all default plugins. Try:

  • Numbered lists and lists starting with - or * should continue automatically.
  • Press tab or shift+tab to indent or outdent the current line.
  • Press option+arrow up or option+arrow down to flip two lines.
  • Cut when nothing is selected to cut the entire line. Also works for copying.
  • Press cmd+shift+d when nothing is selected to duplicate the entire line.
  • Press cmd+shift+k when nothing is selected to delete the entire line.
  • Type / for autocompletions.
